Who We Are

LaVant Consulting, Inc. provides accessible and inclusive content marketing, strategic advising, and training for industry leaders committed to increasing authentic disability representation and fostering meaningful cultures of belonging.

Comprised of a majority disabled, people of color-led team, our intersectional lived experiences, non-judgemental approach, and unique ability to keep up with the fast pace of innovative industries demonstrate the LCI difference.
