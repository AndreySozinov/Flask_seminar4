How Merchant Center works

List your products and business across Google for free

Get started by creating a free Merchant Center account and providing information about your products. If you have a physical location, you can also create a Business Profile to list and manage your local store.

Customers click to purchase your products

Your business and products can appear on Google Search, Maps, and more, when users search for your business or products. Once a customer clicks, you can direct them to your online store, or to a physical location to complete their purchase.

Grow your business with personalized insights

We'll provide actionable insights about how customers interact with your business and products so you can help increase traffic to your site or local store.

Help boost your sales with paid advertising

When you're ready, you can promote your products to reach more customers with advertising that drives sales.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. You can show your products and store on Google for free, just like you can show your website in Google search results at no cost.

When youâre ready, you can supplement your free listings with paid advertising. Paid product ads show up more prominently and can reach even more potential customers. With Google Ads, you choose the goals you want to achieve, and youâll never pay more than your monthly budget cap.

What do I need to sign up for Merchant Center?

You need to be a retailer selling products through a website, in a physical store, or both. To sign up, youâll need a Google account. If you donât have one yet, you can create one for free here. Then go to Merchant Center to tell us about your business, choose where your customers will check out (on your website, on Google, or at your local store), and add your products.

Our beginnerâs guide to Merchant Center can teach you more about getting started and how to manage your presence across Google's platforms.

How do I upload my products to Merchant Center?

If you use Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, PrestaShop or another supported e-commerce platform, you can automatically add your products to Google when you link your e-commerce store to your Merchant Center account. Any future changes you make to products are automatically synced to Google.

If you don't use a supported platform, you can upload your product offerings via a file or spreadsheet, or manually enter individual products.

Where will my products show up?

Once your products are added and confirmed as eligible, they can show up across different Google surfaces, such as Google Search, Google Maps, YouTube, Google Shopping tab and Google Images.

Learn more about where products show and the requirements.

Will Google ship my products?

No. Google helps customers find your products and your store. When they are interested in buying from you, they visit your website and go through your checkout process like any other website visitor. You decide how to ship the products.

How do I get Google Merchant Center support?

Troubleshoot with ease by visiting the Google Merchant Center Help Center. Fix common issues, understand how to measure your results, and more.
