International Women's Day 2023

International Women’s Day 2021 brings together thousands of developers globally as a moment to provide visibility for the incredible contributions of women around the world and is a call to action for accelerating gender equality.

 

During March and April 2021, our Women Techmakers and Google Developer Group communities will come together to celebrate, learn and connect with each other at IWD summits, events and meet-ups. Content can range from technical talks and workshops to inspirational keynotes, career-focused panels or networking opportunities - so there is always something for any developer. Communities are encouraged to share what they have created using #CouragetoCreate, whether it’s new technologies, creative frameworks, positive change, or social justice and equity.

 

Many IWD events will be virtual this year, which means you can be a part of them from anywhere in the world! Use the search functionality below to find events in your local area, or search for technologies or topics you're interested in learning about.

Women Techmakers' International Women's Day (IWD) campaign brings together thousands of developers to celebrate, connect and learn. Globally it's a moment to recognize the incredible contributions of women around the world, and is also a call to action for accelerating gender equity.
 
During March and April, Women Techmakers Ambassadors and Google Developer Group communities will host events around the theme #ProgressNotPerfection. Events range from larger summits to intimate gatherings and cover content from technical talks, workshops or Codelabs to inspirational keynotes, leadership sessions or career panels -- so there's always something for every developer.
