For transitioning service members and veterans

Build new skills for your transition to a civilian career.

Learn how to maximize the skills and experiences you gained in the military to successfully transition to a civilian career.

We collaborated with experts from the Center for Veteran Transition and Integration at Columbia University and FourBlock to create custom resources designed to help you find a job and succeed in the civilian workforce.

Learn how to update your resume for your civilian job search from Sean, a U.S. Army veteran and alumnus of Columbia University who now works as a data scientist at Google.
