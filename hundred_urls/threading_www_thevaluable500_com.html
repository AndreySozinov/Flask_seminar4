Disability  is our  business.

We are the global business partnership of 500 companies working together to end disability exclusion.

Our Work

Synchronised  Collective  Action

We drive change through Synchronised Collective Action. Channelling the power of 500 companies working in harmony – moving in the same direction, at the same time, against the same system barrier.

Unified in our purpose to accelerate inclusion for the one in five people living with a disability, we are transforming business systems to transform society.
