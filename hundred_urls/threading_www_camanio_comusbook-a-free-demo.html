About us

Camanio was founded in 2016 in Stockholm, Sweden, with the aim to improve care through high-tech solutions. We offer innovative digital services that provide integrity, safety and quality of life for everyone – the person in need of care, their family, caregivers and decision makers.

Camanio is part of the AddLife Group consisting of market-leading companies in niche segments within Life Science with offerings aimed primarily at the healthcare sector.
